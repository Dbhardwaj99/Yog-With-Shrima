About

∎

What’s my story? I am a Law Graduate, housewife, and mother of two. Seven years ago, I was struggling with arthritis and thyroid issues, with constant joint pain. Then I discovered yoga and began practicing it. Yoga transformed my life, helping me become the best and fittest version of myself at the age of 45. Inspired by my incredible journey, I started teaching yoga four years ago and have cherished every class since. I proudly hold a Diploma in Yoga and a 200hr Registered Yoga Teacher certification, empowering others to find their own path to health and wellness!

How would I describe my style of teaching? I’ve been fortunate enough to have had exposure and inspiration from a variety of yoga teachers and styles. My main passion lies in Vinyasa Flow, but I also teach a range of styles, including Ashtanga, Power, Restorative and Hatha yoga.

What are my classes like? I offer both offline and online yoga classes to accommodate everyone’s needs. Offline classes take place in my home yoga studio, while online sessions are conducted via Google Meet. I’ve had the pleasure of teaching a diverse range of students from all over the country, as well as my wonderful neighbors. My teaching style is friendly and motivational, creating a supportive and uplifting environment for all my students. Join me to experience a transformative journey in a warm and welcoming community.
